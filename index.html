Quality Assurance

Quality Assurance System

Established in April 2014, our testing center builds on Cormetech's advanced testing methodology and was accredited by CNAS in August 2015.

Equipped with more than 20 internationally recognized testing and laboratory instruments, the center covers the full chain of catalyst inspection, material analysis, and performance verification.

Scope includes appearance, geometry, mechanical performance, process performance, composition analysis, surface characteristics, water quality, and raw-material testing.
01Appearance Inspection
Structural integrity and surface condition checks.
02Geometric Characteristics
Length, dimensions, wall thickness, pore size, pitch, wave height and depth, curvature, geometric surface area, and open area ratio.
03Mechanical Properties
Axial compressive strength, radial compressive strength, adhesive strength, and abrasion resistance.
04Process Performance
DeNOx efficiency, NH3 slip, SO2/SO3 conversion, and activity factor.
05Chemical Composition
Main components including TiO2, V2O5, WO3, MoO3, Al2O3, SiO2, CaO, P2O5, and SO3.
06Trace Elements
K, Na, Ca, P, As, and Pb analysis.
07Surface Characteristics
Specific surface area, microstructure, solid-state structure, active-site distribution, surface acidity, and surface deposits.
08Water Quality
Hardness, alkalinity, chloride, turbidity, conductivity, pH, dissolved oxygen, total iron, total dissolved solids, and related indicators.
09Basic Physical-Chemical Analysis
Ammonia solution concentration, lactic acid content, ammonium metavanadate, ammonium sulfate, ammonium molybdate, PEO, CMC, titanium dioxide, glass fiber, and RP-CHOP testing.
